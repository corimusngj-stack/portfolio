Our Story

BORN FROM A PASSION FOR THE RIDE

Welcome to MotoElite, a motorcycle website created by five Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) students from Pampanga State Agricultural University in Pampanga. Established in 2026, Moto Elite was developed to provide motorcycle enthusiasts with useful information, updates, and insights about different motorcycles, from everyday rides to big bikes.

As students passionate about technology and motorcycles, we aim to create a user-friendly and informative platform where riders and enthusiasts can explore motorcycle brands, models, and the latest trends in the motorcycle community. Moto Elite reflects our dedication to combining innovation, creativity, and our IT knowledge into a practical and engaging web-based project.

Today, Moto Elite continues to grow as a trusted online destination for motorcycle enthusiasts, reaching over 50,000+ monthly riders and visitors. Through our content and community-driven platform, we strive to connect riders, share knowledge, and promote the passion for motorcycles across Pampanga and beyond.
